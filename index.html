Travis Schiller

Travis Schiller (born February 27, 2004) is a college student in Pennsylvania. He has an associate degree in Information Technology from Montgomery County Community College. Travis is currently going to school at Penn State Berks, studying Information Technology.

Schiller played sports throughout college at his first 2 years at "Montco". His second year at Montco he dislocated his shoulder and was unable to finish the season. He is now playing for his current college.

Travis also has 3 younger siblings. He has 2 brothers and 1 sister. His oldest brother is Adam Schiller who is 15 years old. The youngest of his siblings is his 2-year-old brother, and then his 4-year-old sister.

Early life

Schiller was born in Pottstown, Pennsylvania. His mother, Lindsey Schiller (1982–present), had him when she was 20 years old. His father, Daniel Schiller, was 25 at the time. His mother is an RN Nurse at Pottstown Hospital. His father is currently an engineer for Morgan Truck Body.

When he was 12 years old, his parents moved out of Pottstown to Boyertown, Pennsylvania. His mother Lindsey went to school at Reading Area Community College. She obtained her nursing degree in 4 years while taking care of Travis as a baby. His father got his degree in engineering at University of Pittsburgh.

Career

Baseball

Travis played baseball since he was 4 years old. Even at that age, he was out hitting wiffle balls with his father and neighbors. Once he turned 6, he was able to start little league. From there on, he continued playing all the way through middle school and high school. Travis played baseball at Boyertown High School. He was an outfielder who later transitioned into a pitcher. Once he graduated from Boyertown, he played at Montgomery County Community College. His freshman year, he had a batting average of .360. He played the following year but then ran into some injuries. He is now currently playing for Penn State Berks.
